WW1 mod of Domination map.

Fall 1914. With the death of the Arch Duke Franz Ferdinand, Europe is plunged into war.
Titantic armies never seen before muster for battle.
Colonial forces of the great powers skirmish around the world.
Soldiers of all sides are told they will be home for Christmas.
No one suspects they will battle for years over a few miles of no man's land.
