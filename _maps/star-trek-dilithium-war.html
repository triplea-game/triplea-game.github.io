This is a 6 player map with two alliances.

Set 2/3 into the time of Star Trek Deep Space Nine Season 3.
Take control of a Star Trek power and battle in space and on planets.
Randomly placed planets ensure that every playthrough is different.
Introducing Command, Engineering, Science and Dilithium resources.
Use the technology development system to upgrade your units.
Advance in commander rank and get more government support.
