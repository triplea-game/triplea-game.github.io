Arena of Death by TheDog

Introduction
A tactical gladiator style skirmish game with four units a side, in an Arena, 1 v 1.
Heroes/gladiators are 7 tropes, Archer, Barbarian, Priest, Rogue, Summoner, Warrior, Wizard.
The heroes had to have multiple wounds/Hit Points and be magically healed to give an extra dimension to the game.
