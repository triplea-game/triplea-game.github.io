by Frostion

This is an 8 player map with two alliances.

Set in an alternate timeline where you determine the course of history.
Play as part of the European Eastern or Western tribal alliances.
Survive the wildlife, barbarian tribes, the black death and nuclear war.
Advance through the ages while personalizing your tribal build options.
Use the special tech tree system to gain access to specific units.
