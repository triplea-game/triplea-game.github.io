An FFA map with a lot of features, like: terrain, unit classes, regional elements etc.
2-8 players.
Original fantasy-based strategy game. Fully compatible with AI and player versus player.
Music by Torley Wong, edited by Zim Xero.

To enjoy all the custom sounds the designer had choosen,
go to maps folder( engine prefrences, open user maps and savegames folder) and
unpack Elemental_Forces.zip. Download sounds.zip from:
http://www.mediafire.com/download/y7gj9t6afna4ijm/sounds.zip (manually, browser download)
and simply unpack those into your new Elemental_Forces folder from above. Delete
Elemental_Forces.zip when ready.
