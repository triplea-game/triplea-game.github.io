A revised edition of World War II, including artillery support, and destroyer capabilities.
Includes:
1. Revised game as released.
2. LHTR (Larry Harris Tournament Rules) version, which is the way the game was meant to be played for all tournaments.

The Variations Zip includes a 6 Army Free For All variant, but you will need to download that zip separately.
