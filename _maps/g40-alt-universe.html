G40 Alt Universe is for those who love Axis and Allies 1940 Global, but are looking for a new strategic experience.  The war is the same, but occurs in an alternate universe where the Earth’s land formation happened differently.  It’s a return to the early days of pouring over the map and developing strategies in an unfamiliar setting.
