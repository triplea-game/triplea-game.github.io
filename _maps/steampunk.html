Steampunk 1915

b.) Rough overview:
This is the First World War with added Martians. Every nation has special units, map altered in line with fictional locations from period literature. This has Captain Nemo’s Nautilus and Martian Tripods.

The game features
- a large map with hundreds of territories and many factions
- various unit abilities and special units.

c.) General tips:
An extensive manual is provided in the .zip file. Special units can be powerful.
